Discover Our Story

Kunstgalerie Wien is a celebration of creativity, diversity, and the unifying power of art. We're not just an art gallery; we're a global movement that transcends borders and brings artists and art enthusiasts together from around the world.

Our journey began in the heart of immperial Vienna, a city known for its rich cultural heritage. Inspired by this vibrant environment, we set out to create a space where art knows no boundaries, where artists can shine, and where every art lover can find inspiration.

Our Mission

Kunstgalerie Wien is on a mission to connect artists and art enthusiasts worldwide. We empower artists to showcase their talent, share their stories, and find inspiration from diverse artistic expressions no matter where they are in the world.

We believe that art is a universal language, and through our gallery, we aim to foster cultural exchange and dialogue. We're passionate about supporting emerging artists and providing them with a global stage and market.

Additionally, our mission is to enable artists of our choosing to sell their artwork directly through us, regardless of their location. We want to empower artists to reach a broader audience and gain recognition for their work.
